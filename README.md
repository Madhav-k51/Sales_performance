# Sales_performance

I built this interactive sales performance dashboard using power BI to analyze the business performance.

## Key performance indicators (KPI):

- Total Customers : 793 (Distinct)
- Total Orders : 10k (Round)
- Total Sales : 2.30 M (+ 46.9% ^)
- Total Profit : 286k (+ 48.5% ^)

# 📊 Dashboard Insights:
- Sales by Category : Technology leads overall sales (36.4%), followed by Furniture and Office Supplies.
- 
- Top 5 profit by Sub-category : Copiers are generates the highest profit overall the sub-categories, among 56k.

- Top 5 States by Sales: California and New York are the Highest revenue generating sates.

- Top 5 Customers by Sales : A small group of customers contributes significantly to total revenue.

- Monthly Sales Trend : Sales shows the strong growth at end of the year, with peaks in November and September.

- Profit by Segment : The Consumer segment contributes the highest percentage of total profit (46.8%)
